Data Engineering

Data engineering is the discipline focused on designing, building, and maintaining systems that collect, store, process, and deliver data in a reliable and scalable way. It forms the foundation that allows organizations to turn raw data into usable information for analytics, reporting, and machine learning.

Cloud Computing

Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services—such as servers, storage, databases, networking, software, and analytics—over the internet instead of on local computers or physical data centers. It allows individuals and organizations to access powerful technology resources on demand, paying only for what they use.
